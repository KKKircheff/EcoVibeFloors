## Technical Properties

### Avatara Pro
Structure layers:
1. Supermatte soft-touch surface texture
2. Digital printing in HD-Quality
3. Micro-levelling with primer
4. Talcusan® baseboard

### Avatara Perform
Structure layers:
1. Supermatte soft-touch surface texture
2. Elastic functional layer
3. Digital printing in HD-Quality
4. Micro-levelling with primer
5. Talcusan® baseboard
6. SoftSense impact sound insulation (integrated underlay)

**Both series:** Waterproof · Extra matt lacquer finish
**Underfloor heating:** Suitable for hot water and electric underfloor heating systems.
**Note (*Wood decors):** Stone decors have R10 slip resistance.
