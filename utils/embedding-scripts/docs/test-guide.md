## Installation Tips

- Always acclimate flooring to room temperature before installation
- Ensure subfloor is level and dry
- Follow manufacturer guidelines for expansion gaps
- Use appropriate underlayment for your installation method

## Maintenance

Regular cleaning with appropriate products will keep your floors looking beautiful for years to come.
